Who we are?

We are a group of engineering students and entrepreneurs from the UPC. Together we identified a business opportunity related to the tourism sector and receipt of packages from abroad to Peru.

Mision

Be the most efficient and simple national and international parcel delivery service for all types of users..

Vision

Become the preferred package purchase and delivery service for Peruvians, thus surpassing the competition for the quality experience we provide to each of our customers..
